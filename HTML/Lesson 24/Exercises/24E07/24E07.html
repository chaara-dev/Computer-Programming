Vanvouver, British Columbia

A seaport city in western Canada, located in the Lower Mainland region of British Columbia. As the most populous city in the province, the 2016 cencus recorded 631,486 people in the city, up from 603,502 in 2011.
The Greater Vancouver area had a population of 2,463,431 in 2016, making it the third-largest metropolitan area in Canada. Vancouver has the highest population density in Canada, with over 5,400 people per square kilometre, which makes it the fifth-most densely populated city in North America, behind New York City, Guadalajara, San Fransisco, and Mexico City.
